## Membership Eligibility and Joining

Determine if your operations qualify for ASL membership.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Who qualifies for membership?">
    You qualify if you own or operate domestic vessels transporting goods or passengers on the St. Lawrence River, Great Lakes, Arctic regions, or Maritime provinces. ASL serves 14 shipowners focused on these routes since 1936.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What benefits do members receive?" default-open="true">
    Members gain regulatory updates, expertise pooling, position papers on key issues, and connections with government and stakeholders. ASL promotes your interests through information sharing and representation.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Eligibility" icon="check-circle">
    Review your vessel operations against ASL's geographic focus: St. Lawrence, Great Lakes, Arctic, Maritime provinces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Application">
    Email your company details, vessel list, and routes to info@asl-1936.com.

    ```
    Subject: ASL Membership Application

    Dear ASL Team,

    Company: Your Shipping Ltd.
    Vessels: 3 bulk carriers
    Routes: St. Lawrence to Great Lakes

    Best regards,
    Your Name
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Onboarding">
    ASL reviews your submission within 2 weeks and schedules an onboarding call.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Advocacy Processes

ASL advocates for members with authorities and stakeholders.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How does ASL advocate for members?">
    You rely on ASL to monitor regulations, pool expertise, draft positions, and represent interests in meetings with government officials and elected representatives.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What issues does ASL address?">
    Key areas include maritime safety, environmental regulations, infrastructure development, and trade policies affecting St. Lawrence shipping.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Submit your advocacy priorities via the member portal to influence ASL's annual positions.
</Callout>

## Strategic Links and Cooperation

Build connections through ASL's network.
