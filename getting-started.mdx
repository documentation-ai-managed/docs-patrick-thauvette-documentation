## Overview

Welcome to Les Armateurs du Saint-Laurent (ASL). You represent shipowners or stakeholders in maritime transportation on the St. Lawrence River, Great Lakes, Arctic, and Maritime provinces. ASL supports your interests through advocacy, regulatory monitoring, and collaboration since 1936. Follow these steps to join, contact us, and access benefits.

<Callout kind="info">
ASL represents 14 domestic shipowners. Contact us to discuss membership tailored to your operations.
</Callout>

## Apply for Membership

You start by submitting an application. ASL reviews applications quarterly to ensure alignment with our mission of promoting St. Lawrence maritime growth.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Eligibility" icon="check-circle">
    Confirm your operations involve domestic shipping on ASL-covered routes. Visit our [eligibility criteria](https://asl-riverie.org/criteria) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather Documents" icon="file-text">
    Prepare your company profile, vessel registry, and financial summary. Include proof of operations in the St. Lawrence region.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Application" icon="send">
    Email your package to `membership@asl-riverie.org`. Use the template below for your cover letter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attend Interview" icon="video">
    Schedule a virtual meeting with our board. ASL contacts you within 2 weeks of submission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboard" icon="users">
    Pay the annual fee (starting at `{CAD}5,000`) and gain access to member portals and events.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contact ASL and Access Resources

Reach out based on your role. ASL provides tailored resources for quick engagement.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Prospective Members" icon="user-plus">
    Email `info@asl-riverie.org` for application guidance. Download our [membership brochure](https://asl-riverie.org/brochure.pdf).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Stakeholders" icon="users">
    Use our stakeholder portal at `https://portal.asl-riverie.org`. Report regulatory issues via the form.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Media & Public" icon="globe">
    Press inquiries to `media@asl-riverie.org`. Access public reports on maritime statistics.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
